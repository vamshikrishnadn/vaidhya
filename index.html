About Us

We are professionals in various service oriented works from past 25 years now we are launching a new sister concern of Jireh Technologies as Vaidhya.com Vaidhya.com. is a Local Search engine of information technology like medical services that provides Ayurvedic Doctors, General Physicians, Ayurvedic and General medicines, Oxygen, Ambulance, Diagnostic center, Medical Equipments, and many more health related services.
This local search related all kinds of medical services to users across India By doing so, it has transitioned from being purely a Medical information service provider of local search
The organisation aims to make direct communication between Patients and Medicals services through online chat, video call, direct visit & door step visit. Our software is developed to help doctors simplify their workflow, write reports faster and improve efficiency. We want our users (doctors) to spend as much time as possible with patients and less time on accounting or notes, paperwork etc.
